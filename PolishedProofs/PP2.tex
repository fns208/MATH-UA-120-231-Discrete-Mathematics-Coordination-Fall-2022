\section*{Proof Options}

Please choose \textbf{one} of the following exercises. Begin with ``Claim:" and write the statement you intend to prove. Then write ``Proof:" and the proof. You can choose your own end-of-proof marker for flair. \textbf{You may not use Boolean algebra in your proofs.}

$$ \text{ Prove that for all sets } A, B, C,  \text{ we have } A \cap (B \Delta C) = (A \cap B) \; \Delta \; (A \cap C) $$
$$ \text{ \textbf{or} Prove that for all sets } A, B, C,  \text{ we have } A \times (B \Delta C) = (A\times B) \; \Delta \; (A\times C)$$
